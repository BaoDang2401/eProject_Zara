About Us

History

Established in 2006 by Dr. Wade Brackenbury and a dedicated team of associates, Zara Therapy Clinic has locations in Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang and Hanoi with modern facilities to provide international standards of healthcare services. It will provide the community and patients access to the most comprehensive medical care and effective personalized treatment methods of international standard in Vietnam.

Vision

To be a company that attracts, rewards, and retains highly skilled physical therapy staff. Our proactive approach to the development of best business practices and the promotion of effective treatment methods will help us accomplish this. We also take pride in providing exceptional service with advanced technologies to help you reach your optimal well-being as quickly as possible.
